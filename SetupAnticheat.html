THE SETUP

First load up your roblox studio and right click on "Workspace" and click on "Insert from file". Then select the file you downloaded. (It should be named ANTICHEAT.rbxmx) Then insert the file. Next, drag the localscript that was inserted (named "ANTICHEAT") into StarterPlater ---> StarterCharacterScripts. Enjoy!
